Instagram is a social media platform that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers. 
It was launched in 2010 and was later acquired by Facebook in 2012. As of 2021, Instagram has over one billion active users worldwide.

(02)
The platform's mission is to bring people closer together through shared experiences. It aims to inspire creativity and self-expression, 
and to provide a space where users can connect with others who share their interests.
